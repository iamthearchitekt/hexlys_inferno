# Boss Encounter & Falling Platforms

We've extended the end of the level and introduced several new classic platformer mechanics! Here is a breakdown of what was just added to the game.

## The Bowser-Style Boss Encounter
- **The Arena:** The level has been extended with a flat, enclosed Boss Arena right before the portal. The portal has been moved to the very end of this new section.
- **The Boss:** We've imported your two sprite frames and created an invincible, towering skeletal demon boss!
- **Mechanics:** 
  - He paces back and forth guarding the portal.
  - He periodically leaps high into the air.
  - He spits fireballs directly at Hexly.
  - **Strategy:** Because he's invincible, you cannot stomp him or defeat him with fireballs. You must time your movement to sprint *underneath* him when he jumps, or skillfully vault over him while dodging his fireballs to reach the portal behind him!

## Falling Chain Platforms
- During the stressful "flying enemies" section over the lava, the old static chain platforms have been replaced with **Falling Platforms**.
- To make it trickier, the platforms are now scattered at varying heights.
- **Mechanics:** When Hexly lands on a falling platform, it begins to shake violently. After exactly 2 seconds, the platform breaks loose and plummets into the lava! You'll need to keep moving quickly.
